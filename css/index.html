Mumford & Songs

Act one · Act two

Mumford & Sons have celebrated the release of their fifth album, RUSHMERE, with an extensive North American tour for later this year. The North American RUSHMERE arena and amphitheater tour of North America commences on June 5 in the city Bend, Oregon, and concludes a second leg at the CHI Health Center Arena in Omaha, Nebraska on October 26. The supporting acts include Japanese Breakfast, Gregory Alan Isakov, Michael Kiwanuka, Sierra Ferrell, Gigi Perez, Margo Price, Lucius, Madison Cunningham, Good Neighbours, and Divorce.

Billy Corgan Introduces New Solo Project

Act one · Act two

Billy Corgan of solo fame and The Smashing Pumpkins, has announced a new solo project titled Billy Corgan and The Machines of God. This band will commemorate the anniversaries of legendary albums Mellon Collie and The Infinite Sadness and the double album Machina/The Machines of God & Machina II/The Friends & Enemies of Modern Music as well as the 2024 release, Aghori Mhori Mei on the U.S. run.
